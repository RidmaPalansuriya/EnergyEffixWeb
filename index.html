Smart Meter

This feature enables users to easily view real-time meter readings via a bar graph visualization.

Prediction

A machine learning model is utilized to predict the units that will be consumed each month, offering accurate estimations based on historical data.

Goal Setting

Users can set a goal for the number of units they intend to consume in a given month, and if the usage exceeds, the application provides a notification to alert the user.

Bill Generation

Generate the current month's actual bill utilizing smart meter readings, allowing users to conveniently pay the bill through the "pay bill" option integrated within the application.

EnergyEffix Smart Meter

The IoT smart meter is a small device installed on your electrical panel. It acts like a tireless behind-the-scenes hero, constantly monitoring your home's energy consumption in real-time. This device gathers granular data on exactly how much power your appliances and electronics are using.

EnergyEffix Mobile Application

The mobile app is your energy command center,It securely connects to your smart meter, giving you a clear and convenient way to see your electricity usage patterns.By analyzing the data collected by the smart meter, the app identifies areas where you might be using more energy than needed. It then provides personalized tips and suggestions to help you reduce unwanted usage and ultimately lower your electricity bill.

Features

EnergyEffix offers a suite of innovative features designed to empower users in managing their electricity consumption effectively and efficiently.

Real-time Electricity Consumption Monitoring

Users can track their electricity usage in real-time through the Mobile App connected to our customized IoT-based Smart Meter.

  • EnergyEffix keeps you constantly informed with real-time data on your home's electricity usage.
  • Through the user-friendly mobile app, you can monitor your energy consumption at any time, from anywhere.
  • By seeing exactly how much electricity you're using moment-by-moment, you can identify areas for potential reduction.

Predict Your Electricity Usage with Machine Learning

EnergyEffix utilizes the power of machine learning to forecast your future electricity consumption. This innovative feature goes beyond simply monitoring your current usage.

  • This data is then fed into an advanced machine learning model. Over time, the model learns your unique usage patterns
  • Using its knowledge of your patterns, the model generates personalized forecasts of your future electricity consumption.
  • With these predictions in hand, you can proactively manage your energy usage. Plan high-usage activities for off-peak hours or adjust your usage.

Save with Goal Setting & Rewards

EnergyEffix goes beyond just providing insights; it helps you turn those insights into action and savings with a fun and engaging approach.

  • You can set specific, achievable goals for reducing your electricity consumption.
  • The app keeps you on track by providing clear visualizations of your progress towards your goals. This allows you to see the impact of your efforts and stay motivated.
  • As you achieve your goals, you'll be rewarded.
  • The app features a leaderboard where you can see how your energy-saving efforts stack up against others in your community. This friendly competition can add an extra layer of motivation to keep you striving for improvement.

Bill Management at Your Fingertips

Stay organized and informed with EnergyEffix's convenient bill management features.

  • Access and view your past electricity bills directly within the app.
  • Easily monitor your electricity expenses over time. This transparency empowers you to identify trends and make informed decisions about your energy usage..
  • For LECO and CEB customers, the app provides quick links to their official websites, allowing you to easily view and pay your bills directly through their secure platforms.
